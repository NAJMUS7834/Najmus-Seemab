About

Abu Dhabi Commercial Bank PJSC, commonly called ADCB, is a bank in the United Arab Emirates. Abu Dhabi Commercial Bank was formed in 1985 as a public shareholding company with limited liability, following the mergers between Emirates Commercial Bank, Federal Commercial Bank, and Khaleej Commercial Bank, which was established in 1975.
